3D Slicer In My Language

3D Slicer is an open-source software platform for medical imaging data analysis, 3D visualization, and image-guided therapy used around the world. However, the application is available in the English language only. Through funding support from the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, we have created the 3D Slicer Internationalization initiative to ensure that scientists, researchers, and students worldwide can realize the full potential of the platform.
